Oadie

We're Oadie!

Oadie is a "closet punk" band from San Diego, California. Oadie started as a series of songs written by Kevin Rogers while in college but it wasn't until August of 2016 that a full band was assembled and songs finished and recorded. Shortly after, freshman EP "Stranger Station" was released to the world. So far, the EP has gone fairly unnoticed, but Travis' mom said she enjoys it so we're content with it.

  • Kevin Rogers- Main Vocals, Rhythm Guitar.
  • Travis Galarza- Backup Vocals, Drums.
  • Mikey Byous- Bass Guitar.
  • Jeremy Sheahan- Lead Guitar.
*Closet Punk: Rock music that isn't quite punk, but really wants to be.
